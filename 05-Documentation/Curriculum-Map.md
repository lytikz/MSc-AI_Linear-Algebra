**Project:** MSc-AI Linear Algebra
**Brand:** lytikz.ai

## Overview

45 sessions across 12 weeks. Weeks 1 to 9 follow the full four-session
weekly arc defined in Learning-Model-Spec.md. Weeks 10 to 12 are
intentionally lighter in structure, shifting from concept acquisition
to synthesis, connection, and readiness — each containing two lessons
and one Q&A. All lessons follow the structure defined in
Learning-Model-Spec.md.

Lessons already built are marked as complete.
